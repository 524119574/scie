Our vision and goal

This page explains SCIE's mission and vision.

Mission Statement

The primary purpose of SCIE is to educate students in a challenging and creative international environment, thereby enabling them to attain the highest possible level of achievement in secondary education. SCIE strives to open the minds of students to knowledge, to encourage creativity and curiosity, and to enable students to take best advantage of their future educational opportunities.

Vision Statement

To be the most successful IGCSE and A level centre in China

Educational Objective

Students should leave SCIE having acquired:

  • The confidence to become a successful global citizen
  • Sufficient maturity and personal autonomy to live independently
  • Knowledge of, and confidence in, their own abilities
  • Skills for independent learning
  • A respect for cultural diversity
  • A place at a respectable university appropriate for their abilities and interests
  • A passion for learning
  • High standards of honesty and personal integrity
  • A strong positive regard for the College, staff and students
  • Intellectual curiosity and creativity
  • An understanding of, and empathy for, other people’s points of view
  • The ability to communicate think and analyse in English
  • Opportunities to be a leader and to work in a team
